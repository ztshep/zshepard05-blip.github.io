UConn Engineering · ISR

Human Powered Submarine Club

We design, build, and race a human-powered submarine for the International Submarine Races (ISR). Hands-on engineering: CAD, composites, testing, and real performance iteration.

Join on GroupMe Join on UConntact

What we do

Real Engineering

CAD, CFD, controls, and structural design — plus documentation and design reviews like a real program.

Fabrication

Composites, machining, 3D printing, assembly, and test iteration. Build it, break it, improve it.

Testing & Diving

Pool testing, safety systems, procedures, and diver support — turning a design into a race-ready vehicle.
